**Footnotes:**

* **Trailing P/E:** The ratio of the current stock price to the earnings per share over the last 12 months.
* **Forward P/E:** The ratio of the current stock price to the estimated earnings per share for the next 12 months.
* **PEG Ratio:** The ratio of the P/E ratio to the expected earnings growth rate.
* **Trailing EPS:** The earnings per share over the last 12 months.
* **Forward EPS:** The estimated earnings per share for the next 12 months.
* **Trailing Annual Dividend Yield:** The annualized dividend yield based on the last dividend declared.
* **Profit Margin:** The percentage of revenue that is left over after subtracting expenses.
* **Gross Margin:** The percentage of revenue that is left over after subtracting the cost of goods sold.
* **Operating Margin:** The percentage of revenue that is left over after subtracting the cost of goods sold and operating expenses.
* **Return on Assets:** The percentage of return on the company's total assets.
* **Return on Equity:** The percentage of return on the company's shareholders' equity.
* **Debt-to-Equity Ratio:** The ratio of the company's total debt to its total equity.
* **Current Ratio:** The ratio of the company's current assets to its current liabilities.
* **Quick Ratio:** The ratio of the company's liquid assets to its current liabilities.
* **Trailing 12-Month Price-to-Sales Ratio:** The ratio of the current stock price to the company's revenue over the last 12 months.
* **Trailing 12-Month EV/Revenue:** The ratio of the company's enterprise value to its revenue over the last 12 months.
* **Trailing 12-Month EV/EBITDA:** The ratio of the company's enterprise value to its earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization over the last 12 months.
* **52-Week Change:** The percentage change in the stock price over the last 52 weeks.
* **S&P 500 52-Week Change:** The percentage change in the S&P 500 index over the last 52 weeks.
